About BGE Department

The Department of BGE focuses on biotechnology, genetic engineering, genomics, microbiology, molecular biology, tissue culture, vaccine science, agriculture & bio-tech industries. Students engage in lectures, lab classes, seminars, research methodology, project work and publications. Graduates are prepared for careers in agriculture biotechnology, vaccine/biopharma, genetic engineering, genomics, environmental biotechnology, and industrial bioprocessing.
